Person signs up the first time at the website. This gives them a username and password and a user account in our system. Their *cloud* database name is just a millisecond number time and date stamp (but turned into an integer). The first time they log into the app: The app initializes a family database, and tries to get primary user. If successful, brings up the login modal showing all users for the family. If not successful, asks for the user's email.
